Marina Service Technician

Chester, MD
Full Time - 40 hours/week
Starting at $25/hour

Job Description

The Pocket Yacht Company is now seeking a full-time Marina Service Technician to join our growing team. This qualified individual must be able to problem-solve, work well in a team environment, and be efficient to achieve high customer satisfaction. Responsibilities include:

  • Service boats to a high standard.
  • Properly diagnose and repair boats brought in for warranty or service.
  • Able to perform routine service inspections on inboard and outboard engines.
  • Troubleshoot, repair, and install electronics in boats.
  • Ability to winterize boats.
  • Work under the direction of the Service Manager to complete work in a timely manner.
  • Maintain knowledge of Marina technology. Has or is willing to acquire Yamaha, Volvo Penta, and ABYC certification.
  • Ensure all tools and equipment are maintained and in good working condition and maintain a clean workplace.
  • Ability to transport trailers and boats to satellite offices.
